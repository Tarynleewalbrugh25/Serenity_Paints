ABOUT

Welcome to Serenity, a place where creativity and expression meet!. We have a wide range of artwork crafted by The worlds most Talented Artists, each painting embodies unique narratives and emotions that the artist would like to express. This platform was generally created to celebrate not only established artists but emerging ones as well,the perfect place to showcase their art. Serenity was founded in 2023 by Ceo Taryn-lee Walbrugh at age 23. what started out as a gag idea only creating the platform to showcase her skills in developing a completed website. Through that Serenity was created and it truly changed may artists lives!
